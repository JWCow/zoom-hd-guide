Enabling HD Video Quality in Zoom

Important Notice: Zoom implements default video quality limitations on standard accounts to optimize bandwidth usage. To achieve maximum video quality, it is necessary to complete both the client-side configuration and submit an account activation request through Zoom Support.

Section 1: Client-Side Configuration

Step 1: Accessing Video Settings

1. Visit Zoom Web Settings Portal

2. Sign in to your Zoom account if prompted

3. In the left sidebar, click on "Settings"

4. Navigate to "Meeting" tab

5. Scroll down to "In Meeting (Advanced)"

6. Look for "Meeting HD Video Quality" toggle

Step 2: HD Configuration

1. Within the "In Meeting (Advanced)" section, locate the "Meeting-HD Video Quality" option

2. Toggle the switch to enable HD video

Note: Enhanced video quality settings will increase bandwidth requirements for both meeting hosts and participants.

Section 2: Account-Level Activation

Step 1: Initiating Support Request

1. Visit the Zoom Support Portal

2. Scroll down to the bottom of the page

3. Look for and click the "Submit a Ticket" button

4. You will be redirected to the support ticket form

Step 2: Support Ticket Submission

Complete the support ticket with the following specifications:

Issue Type:

Select "Technical Support"

Priority Level:

Select "Normal" - This is appropriate for feature activation requests

Subject Line:

Copy and paste exactly: "HD Video Quality Activation Request"

Description:

Copy and paste the following template:

"I am writing to request the activation of full HD video capability for my Zoom account. I have configured the client-side HD settings and would like to request the necessary account-level activation to enable optimal video quality for my professional meetings. Thank you for your assistance in this matter."
